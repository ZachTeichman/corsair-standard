Corsair Standard

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What Corsair Standard Is

Corsair Standard is a deterministic formatting compliance tool for Microsoft Word DOCX resume files. It analyzes document structure, including margins, tab stops, spacing, fonts, section order, and typography, and returns a compliance report.

Corsair Standard does not evaluate candidate quality, score applicants, or make recruiting decisions. It checks formatting only.

What We Collect

When you upload a DOCX file, we receive the file temporarily for analysis and basic request metadata, such as IP address, timestamp, and file size, for security and rate limiting.

We do not collect your name, email address, or account information unless you contact us directly.

How We Use Uploaded Files

Uploaded DOCX files are used for one purpose only: running the formatting compliance analysis you requested.

  • We do not store your resume permanently.
  • We do not share your resume with third parties.
  • We do not use your resume to train AI or machine learning models.
  • We do not analyze resume content for candidate evaluation.
  • We do not link resume content to any identity.

Data Retention

Uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of upload. In practice, most files are deleted sooner after analysis is complete and the annotated copy is downloaded.

We do not maintain a long-term archive of uploaded resumes.

Annotated DOCX Files

When you run an audit, we generate an annotated copy of your DOCX with Word-native formatting comments. This file is stored temporarily alongside the original and deleted on the same schedule: within 24 hours.

No AI Training

Uploaded resumes are never used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI or machine learning model. Your document is processed by deterministic rule-based analysis only.

Security

  • Files are stored with randomized identifiers, not your original filename.
  • Files are never executed. They are parsed as XML only.
  • Macro-enabled files (.docm) are rejected at upload.
  • Files are deleted automatically within 24 hours.
  • HTTPS is required for all connections.

No security system is perfect. We recommend not uploading documents containing sensitive personal information beyond what a standard resume contains.

Third-Party Services

If you use the Microsoft Office Viewer preview feature, your annotated DOCX file is temporarily accessible via a public URL so Microsoft's viewer service can render it. This URL expires when the file is deleted. We do not control Microsoft's handling of files accessed through their viewer.

Children

Corsair Standard is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this document.
